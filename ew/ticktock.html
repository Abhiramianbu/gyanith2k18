TICK – TOCK

This three-stage event is primarily focused on detecting and deactivating a prototype bomb circuit. The participants are allocated a particular area where there is a timed charge programmed to blow off within a specified time. The clock is ticking, any wrong move might set off the charge, something smart might gain you more time, some logical thinking might win you a grand cash price. The prerequisites are nothing more than a basic circuit knowledge. The event tests and enhances the participants circuit knowledge, logical thinking and problem-solving ability. The first person to race against time to diffuse the bomb wins the event (No actual explosives involved ;-) ) .

Number of days : 1
Timing: 5 hours
Entry fee : Rs 200/-
Venue : Science Block , NITPy

Rules and Regulations

• It's a team event, where a team consists of maximum 3 members.
• The event spans to three stages, where Preliminary (Stage - I) is tentative.
• The participants are given a kit, which aids in their detection and diffusion, which they    are expected to return without any damage.
• Primary judgement criteria will be the time factor, the first team to solve the circuit will    be given maximum points.
• The rest are awarded points on the level of advancement on solving the circuit.
• Top Teams are qualified for further rounds.
• Diffusing the circuit should solely be on technical and logical basis, any damage to       the circuit will lead to disqualification.
• Different teams, are given different circuits of same complexity.
• Time span of each round extends until the first person resolves the circuit, or till the     max specified limit for each round (varies with each round).
• Teams eliminated in successive rounds won't be given second chance.
