OpenAI


Interested in AI? But don’t know where to start? Come join Artificial Intelligence workshop at GYANITH’18 and build your very own AI! Artificial Intelligence has taken the world by storm from ALEXA to SELF DRIVING CARS. Sadly Artificial Intelligence is filled with a lot of technical jargons such as convoluted neural networks, deep learning, q-learning, machine learning, deep neural networks and the list keeps going on… So get ready to take your first step towards with us by building an AI that learns to drive a car and who can play an old classic game DOOM!
